## 🎯 Quick Start (3 Steps)

### Step 1: Open Scanner
Visit on your phone: **`attendai.vercel.app/scan`**

Type your UID (e.g., `24BCS10001`)

## 📱 Detailed Instructions

### Method 1: Type URL (Simplest)

1. **Open Browser** on your phone
   - Chrome, Safari, or any browser

2. **Type URL**
   ```
   attendai.vercel.app/scan
   ```

3. **Enter Your UID**
   - Example: `24BCS10001`
   - Click "Continue to Scanner"

4. **Start Camera**
   - Click "Start Camera Scanner"
   - Allow camera access (one-time only)

5. **Scan QR Code**
   - Point phone at teacher's screen
   - Hold steady for 1-2 seconds
   - Automatic detection (no button press needed)

6. **Success!**
   - Green message appears
   - Shows subject, teacher, room
   - Attendance marked! ✅

---

### Method 2: Scan Classroom QR Code (Easiest)

1. **Find QR Code Poster** in your classroom

2. **Scan with Phone Camera**
   - Open camera app
   - Point at QR code
   - Tap notification that appears

3. **Scanner Opens Automatically**
   - Already at the right page!

4. **Enter Your UID**
   - Type your UID
   - Click "Continue"

5. **Scan Teacher's QR Code**
   - Point at teacher's screen
   - Done! ✅

---

## ⚠️ Important Notes

### Camera Permission
- **First time**: Browser asks for camera access
- **Click "Allow"** (one-time only)
- **If denied**: Go to browser settings → Site permissions → Camera → Allow

### QR Code Timing
- QR codes **refresh every 5 seconds**
- Always scan the **latest code** on screen
- If scan fails, wait for next refresh and try again

### One Attendance Per Lecture
- You can only mark attendance **once per lecture**
- If you try again, you'll see: "Already marked"
- This prevents duplicate entries

### Internet Required
- You need **internet connection** (WiFi or mobile data)
- Works on any network (not just university WiFi)

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### Problem: Camera Not Working

**Solution 1: Check Permissions**
- Go to browser settings
- Find site permissions
- Enable camera for attendai.vercel.app

**Solution 2: Try Different Browser**
- Chrome (recommended)
- Safari (for iPhone)
- Firefox

**Solution 3: Check HTTPS**
- URL should start with `https://`
- Camera only works on secure sites

### Problem: QR Code Not Scanning

**Solution 1: Lighting**
- Make sure room has good lighting
- Avoid glare on teacher's screen

**Solution 2: Distance**
- Hold phone 20-30 cm from screen
- Not too close, not too far

**Solution 3: Stability**
- Hold phone steady
- Wait 2-3 seconds
- Let camera focus

**Solution 4: Wait for Refresh**
- QR codes refresh every 5 seconds
- Wait for new code and try again

### Problem: "Invalid QR Code" Error

**Reasons:**
- QR code expired (older than 10 seconds)
- Wrong QR code (not from AttendAI)
- Network delay

**Solution:**
- Wait for QR code to refresh
- Scan the new code
- Check internet connection

### Problem: "Already Marked" Message

**Reason:**
- You already marked attendance for this lecture

**Note:**
- This is normal behavior
- Prevents duplicate entries
- Your attendance is already recorded ✅
